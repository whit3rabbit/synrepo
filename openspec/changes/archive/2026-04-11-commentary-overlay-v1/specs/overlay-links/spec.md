## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Define evidence-verified cross-link candidates
synrepo SHALL define a cross-link candidate as a proposed relationship between two graph nodes that is generated by the synthesis pipeline, verified against cited evidence, scored for confidence, and stored in the overlay as non-authoritative until a human declaration promotes it to the graph. Cross-links never enter the graph directly from the pipeline.

#### Scenario: Generate a candidate cross-link
- **WHEN** the synthesis pipeline infers a possible relationship between a code node and a prose node with cited evidence
- **THEN** the overlay stores the candidate with: source nodes, inferred relationship kind, confidence score, cited evidence spans, and full provenance
- **AND** the stored candidate is labeled non-authoritative and does not modify the graph

#### Scenario: Reject a candidate with no verifiable evidence
- **WHEN** the synthesis pipeline proposes a cross-link but cannot produce cited evidence
- **THEN** the overlay contract requires the candidate to be discarded or held at the lowest confidence tier
- **AND** the response does not surface an unverified candidate as probable

### Requirement: Define confidence scoring for cross-link candidates
synrepo SHALL define a confidence scoring model for cross-link candidates that ranks them by the strength and quality of their cited evidence. Confidence tiers determine surfacing behavior: high-confidence candidates may be surfaced to agents; low-confidence candidates are review-queue-only; below-threshold candidates are withheld.

#### Scenario: Surface a high-confidence candidate to an agent
- **WHEN** an agent requests cross-link context and a high-confidence candidate exists with fresh provenance
- **THEN** the response includes the candidate under its overlay source-store label with its confidence tier
- **AND** the candidate is not presented as a graph-backed fact

#### Scenario: Withhold a below-threshold candidate
- **WHEN** a cross-link candidate falls below the confidence threshold
- **THEN** the overlay contract withholds the candidate from normal responses
- **AND** the candidate remains stored for audit and potential re-evaluation

### Requirement: Define cross-link review and promotion workflow
synrepo SHALL define a review workflow for proposed cross-links in curated mode, including a human-review queue, promotion from proposed link to human-declared graph fact, and rejection with audit trail. Promoted links enter the graph as `EdgeKind::Governs` or another declared edge kind with `HumanDeclared` epistemic label, not as overlay content.

#### Scenario: Promote a reviewed cross-link to the graph
- **WHEN** a human reviewer accepts a proposed cross-link through the review workflow
- **THEN** the accepted link is written to the graph as a human-declared edge with appropriate epistemic label
- **AND** the overlay candidate record is updated to reflect the promotion outcome and timestamp

#### Scenario: Reject a proposed cross-link
- **WHEN** a human reviewer rejects a proposed cross-link
- **THEN** the overlay records the rejection with reviewer identity and timestamp
- **AND** the rejected candidate is excluded from future surfacing unless explicitly re-evaluated

### Requirement: Define cross-link audit trail
synrepo SHALL maintain an audit trail for all cross-link candidates, including generation provenance, confidence score history, review decisions, and promotion or rejection outcomes. The audit trail is queryable and survives candidate deletion.

#### Scenario: Audit a promoted cross-link
- **WHEN** an operator queries the audit trail for a link that was promoted to the graph
- **THEN** synrepo returns the original candidate provenance, confidence score, reviewer decision, and promotion timestamp
- **AND** the audit record is independent of whether the overlay candidate entry still exists
